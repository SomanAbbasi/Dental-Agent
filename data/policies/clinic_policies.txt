BRIGHTSMILE DENTAL CLINIC — OFFICIAL POLICIES

=== APPOINTMENT BOOKING ===
Appointments can be booked by calling the clinic or through our AI receptionist.
Each patient receives a unique token number in the format D-XXX upon confirmation.
Token numbers are assigned sequentially and cannot be transferred to another patient.
Walk-in patients are accommodated based on slot availability after booked patients.

=== CANCELLATION POLICY ===
Appointments must be cancelled at least 2 hours before the scheduled time.
Same-day cancellations with less than 2 hours notice are marked as late cancellations.
Patients with 3 or more late cancellations may be asked to prepay for future appointments.
To cancel, call the clinic directly at the reception number.

=== MISSED APPOINTMENT POLICY ===
If a patient misses their appointment without cancelling, it is marked as a no-show.
After 2 no-shows, the patient must call the clinic to discuss rebooking.
No-show slots are released to walk-in patients after 15 minutes past the scheduled time.

=== EMERGENCY DENTAL CARE ===
For dental emergencies such as severe pain, broken teeth, or facial swelling,
call the clinic immediately and inform the receptionist it is an emergency.
Emergency patients are seen on the same day whenever possible.
If the clinic is closed, visit the nearest hospital emergency department.

=== CLINIC HOURS ===
Monday to Saturday: 9:00 AM to 7:00 PM
Sunday: Closed
Public holidays: Closed unless announced otherwise.
Peak hours are 9:00 AM to 11:00 AM and 4:00 PM to 6:00 PM.

=== PAYMENT POLICY ===
Payment is due at the time of the appointment.
Accepted methods: Cash, JazzCash, EasyPaisa, bank transfer.
No insurance billing is handled at the clinic directly.
Receipts are provided for all payments.

=== PATIENT RECORDS ===
Patient records are kept confidential and are not shared with third parties.
Patients may request a copy of their records by visiting the clinic in person.
Records are retained for a minimum of 5 years as per local health regulations.

=== CHILDREN AND MINORS ===
Patients under 18 must be accompanied by a parent or legal guardian.
The guardian must provide consent before any treatment begins.

=== COVID AND HYGIENE PROTOCOLS ===
All staff wear masks and gloves during procedures.
Treatment rooms are sanitized between every patient.
Patients with active fever or respiratory symptoms are asked to reschedule.

=== REFERRALS ===
If a patient requires specialist care beyond general dentistry,
the clinic will provide a written referral to the appropriate specialist.
Referral fees are not charged.
