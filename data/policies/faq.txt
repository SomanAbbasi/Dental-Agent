BRIGHTSMILE DENTAL CLINIC — FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Q: How long does a typical appointment take?
A: A standard checkup takes 20 to 30 minutes. Procedures such as fillings or
extractions may take 45 to 60 minutes. The doctor will inform you of the
estimated duration when you arrive.

Q: Can I bring someone with me to my appointment?
A: Yes, one accompanying person is allowed in the waiting area.
Due to space constraints, only the patient enters the treatment room
unless the patient is a minor or requires assistance.

Q: What should I do if I am running late?
A: Please call the clinic as soon as possible. If you are more than
15 minutes late, your slot may be given to a waiting patient and
you will need to reschedule.

Q: Is there parking available?
A: Limited parking is available outside the clinic.
Street parking is also available nearby.

Q: Do you offer payment plans?
A: Payment plans are not currently available. Full payment is required
at the time of the appointment.

Q: How do I get my token number if I lose it?
A: Call the clinic with your registered phone number and the receptionist
will confirm your token and appointment details.

Q: Can I book an appointment for someone else?
A: Yes, you can book on behalf of a family member.
Please provide their name and phone number during booking.

Q: What languages does the clinic staff speak?
A: Clinic staff speak Urdu, Punjabi, and English.

Q: How do I provide feedback about my experience?
A: You can speak to the clinic manager directly or call the main number
after your visit. All feedback is welcomed and reviewed.
